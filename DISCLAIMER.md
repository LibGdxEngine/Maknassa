## What Maknassa is

Maknassa is a **community-moderation tool for creators**. It runs entirely on
**your own computer**, using **your own web browser session and your own login**,
to help you review the people who interacted with your posts and moderate your own
audience — for example, blocking accounts that harass you.

There is no server: nobody operates Maknassa on your behalf, and your social-media
login/session never leaves your machine. It is not uploaded, stored, or sent
anywhere.

- Automating actions on a third-party platform **may violate that platform's terms

- Do **not** use Maknassa to harass, stalk, target, or harm others, or for any
